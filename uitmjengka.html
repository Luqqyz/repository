My Education

UNIVERSITI TEKNOLOGI MARA KAMPUS JENGKA

My journey as an university student began at the UiTM. I proceeded to the registration session on August 25, 2019.
I ended up taking the business faculty for 6 months, which is equivalent to 1 semester, since I decided to continue the real world of diploma
after finish with my pre-diploma studies. This programme is only offered by UiTM to encourage the Malaysians in furthering their studies
to a more significant level. Aside from that, my experience there was very intriguing on the grounds that I was capable of learning to be self - reliant
and adapt to different environments. Our class has 25 students in total, where I am the class leader between all of classmates. It is not simple once we
take a position where others put their faith in us; simply put, we must demonstrate that we can put forth a valiant effort and be able to face the challenges
and hurdles that lie ahead. I finished my pre-diploma on January 8, 2020, by taking the exam face-to-face on that day and it was our last day before everyone
went to their respective homes. It was a very sad day for all of us as we finally finished our pre-diploma studies and even started to distance ourselves
from each other; who share a lot of nostalgia together. Finally, I obtained a CGPA of 3.27 and was able to continue my studies to the diploma level.
I hope I can see my friends again.
