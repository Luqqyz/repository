My Education

SEKOLAH KEBANGSAAN TAMAN MIDAH (2)

I began in primary school from standard 1 in (2008) to standard 6 (2013) at Sekolah Kebangsaan Taman Midah 2
situated at Jalan Midah 12, Taman Midah, Cheras 56000 Kuala Lumpur SKTM2. In addition to myself, two of my siblings
graduated from the school. Numerous memorable experiences occurred when I was there, such as the yearly sports
day, carnival day, haunted house day, dental check-up day, and daily morning assembly. Memories that are difficult to
locate in the current day. Ultimately, all our younger siblings completed school and continued their education at the same high school..
